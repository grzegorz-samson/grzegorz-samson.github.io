Open workshops | 2026-02-27

Invitation to open workshops focused on generative technologies in sound creation and the basics of VST plugin design.

  • Date: 2026-02-27 (Friday).
  • Venue: New Campus of the Academy of Music, room 235.
  • Time: 17:00-19:45.

Location (map): New Campus of the Academy of Music in Bydgoszcz

Open map

Materials

Workshops

17:00-18:15

Creative use of generative technologies in sound creation

A workshop focused on practical use of generative models in sound work, multimodal content processing, and experimental creative workflows.

  • Transformers in multimodal processing and their role in creative workflows.
  • Practical examples of generative models in sound creation.
  • Audience profile: sound direction, sound design, composition, and music theory.

18:30-19:45

Introduction to VST plugin design for generative sound

A practical workshop featuring the custom generative granular synthesizer, panel architecture, and the basics of audio plugin development workflow.

  • Demo of the custom plugin and VST tool design approach.
  • Introduction to JUCE and tools for creating and modifying plugins.
  • Recommended setup: Visual Studio Code, JUCE, Git, or GitHub Desktop.
