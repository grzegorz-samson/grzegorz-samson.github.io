Full biographical note

Grzegorz Samson has completed a series of artistic-research projects and activities in new sound technologies, with particular emphasis on artificial intelligence, generativity, and interactivity in music environments.

One of the key initiatives was implementing AI solutions within the project carried out at the Academy of Music in Bydgoszcz: "Inclusiveness, Support, New Technologies - improving language and social competences of the Academy community" , co-financed by KPO. He was responsible for developing and implementing interactive AI tutors that support language education for musicians in specialist English.

In artistic practice and project presentations, he presented an interactive multimedia project at the National Multimedia May Festival organized by the Fryderyk Chopin University of Music, and at the Audio Engineering Society European Convention 2025 in Warsaw, where he presented an artistic-research project based on interconnected generative visual-audial systems.

Grzegorz Samson actively publishes scientific papers in recognized international journals, focusing on generative sound design, audiovisual interactivity, and the use of artificial intelligence in creative processes. These works address both practical and theoretical aspects of AI-driven sound content generation and exploration of relationships between media.
