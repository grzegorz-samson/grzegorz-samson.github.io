VST Plugin

The main outcome is a custom VST plugin for generative sound design, developed as part of project tasks focused on prototyping, testing, and documentation.

  • Real-time generation and modulation of granular structures.
  • Workflow focused on experimental sound design and compositional practice.
  • Public overview of features, panels, and the test-build download process.
Go to VST Plugin tab

Composition and audio material

The artistic part of the project includes the piece “Pendamonika”, developed in parallel with GreggularVST and focused on granular processing, layered textures, and an experimental approach to sonic form.

  • The composition was created as a practical outcome of creative testing with the plugin.
  • The material documents the use of the tool in composition workflow and sound design practice.
  • The SoundCloud release serves as a public presentation of the artistic outcome of the project.
Open on SoundCloud

Research and ADC 2025 conference

The project was presented during Audio Developer Conference 2025 (November 10-12, 2025, Bristol, UK and online).

The “From Text Wishes to Sound” poster presents an approach combining prompt-layer design, audio material generation, and real-time granular processing.

Workshops

The dissemination part of the project included workshops focused on creative generative audio and an introduction to working with VST plugins.

  • Presentation of workflow based on generative and granular tools.
  • Overview of plugin panels and practical use cases in music-oriented environments.
  • Workshop materials and documentation are gathered in a dedicated section of the site.
