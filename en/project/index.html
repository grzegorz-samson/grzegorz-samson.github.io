Outcome 1

VST Plugin

The main outcome is a custom VST plugin for generative sound design, developed as part of project tasks focused on prototyping, testing, and documentation.

  • Real-time generation and modulation of granular structures.
  • Workflow focused on experimental sound design and compositional practice.
  • Public overview of features, panels, and test-build download process.

Outcome 3

Research and ADC 2025 conference

The project was presented during Audio Developer Conference 2025 (November 10-12, 2025, Bristol, UK and online).

The "From Text Wishes to Sound" poster presents an approach combining prompt-layer design, audio material generation, and real-time granular processing.
